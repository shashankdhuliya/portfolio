I'm Shashank Dhuliya and I'm a

Shashank Dhuliya is a dedicated lawyer and social worker committed to justice and community development. He is the Founder and CEO of Help People To Save People Trust, a national non-profit organization working in the fields of education, healthcare, and human rights. As an Advocate at the Allahabad High Court, he actively handles cases related to social justice and public welfare. He is passionate about using his legal expertise to empower marginalized communities and promote social change. Through his work, he aims to create a more equitable and just society for all.
